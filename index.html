Elephant Rock State Park

Elephant rock state park is beautiful small-medium state park in southern Missouri. It's 1 mile paved trail is fully accesible for wheelchairs and includes braille directional, and information singage making it accessible to hikers with with visor or mobility imparments. It's name comes from the giant granite formations that formed 1.5 billion years ago.

Don Robinson State Park

Don Robinson State Park is dominated by sandstone formations that have been worn away by water over time to create a stunning canyon which follows one of the trails.

Garden of the Gods State Park

Garden of the Gods state park is located in southwest Illinois.
